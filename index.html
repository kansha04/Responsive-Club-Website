Welcome to Our Universe

The Stargazers Society, Riverside’s premier astronomy club, welcomes stargazers of all levels since 2018. Whether a beginner with a telescope or an experienced astrophotographer, you’ll find a place among our members.

About Our Club

Founded by friends during a meteor shower viewing party, the Stargazers Society has grown into a vibrant community of over 85 members. We believe the night sky belongs to everyone and aim to make astronomy accessible, educational, and magical for our community.
